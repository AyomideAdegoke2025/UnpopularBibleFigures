Unpopular People in the Bible

Discover the untold stories of lesser-known figures.

Joab

Joab was the commander of King David's army. Despite his loyalty, he was known for his ruthlessness and rebellion against the king.

Jezebel

Jezebel was a queen known for her wickedness, leading Israel into idolatry and influencing her husband, King Ahab.

Cain

Cain, the firstborn son of Adam and Eve, became notorious for committing the first murder by killing his brother Abel.
